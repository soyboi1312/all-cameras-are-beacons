! Erasing will unpair Bluetooth

Erase wipes the board's stored Bluetooth bond. If this device was paired with your phone (the All Cameras Are Beacons app), you'll need to remove the existing pairing in your phone's Bluetooth settings and pair again — otherwise the app won't reconnect. A normal flash without erase keeps the pairing.
ACAB
All Cameras Are Beacons

Counter-surveillance detection firmware for the Colonel Panic OUI-Spy and Mesh-Detect boards (XIAO ESP32-S3). Plug the board into USB, pick a build, and flash it from this page. No toolchain is required.

ACAB is passive detect-and-report only. It listens for the advertisements these devices already broadcast, with no jamming, spoofing, or interference.
! Web flashing needs the Web Serial API, so use Chrome, Edge, or Opera on desktop. Safari and Firefox do not work. The board must be connected by USB-C.

OUI-Spy APP

An app-controlled scanner that streams detections to the All Cameras Are Beacons iOS app over BLE and beeps a distinct tone for each target class.

  • BLE + WiFi scanning
  • ACAB GATT service
  • buzzer / LED alerts

The board pairs with the All Cameras Are Beacons iPhone app for live detections and controls. Get it free on TestFlight via the README.

Browser not supported. Use Chrome or Edge. Must be served over HTTPS or localhost.

Mesh-Detect MESH

A standalone scanner that uplinks labelled alerts through a wired Heltec V3 Meshtastic node on a channel you choose.

  • BLE + WiFi scanning
  • Meshtastic uplink
  • private-channel alerts

Plug USB-C into the XIAO Seeed ESP32-S3, the small scanner board, not the Heltec V3. This build flashes the XIAO; flash the Heltec V3 separately with Meshtastic firmware.

Public puts detections on the main LongFast channel (anyone nearby sees them). Private puts them on an encrypted acab channel only your nodes can read, and also needs the Heltec set up to match (see the mesh setup guide).

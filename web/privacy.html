All Cameras Are Beacons Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2026

Short version: All Cameras Are Beacons collects nothing. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking. Your detections stay on your device. The only time the app uses the network is to load map tiles when you open the map.

What the app does

All Cameras Are Beacons is a companion app for the OUI-Spy detection board. It connects to the board over Bluetooth and shows the public radio signals that nearby devices already broadcast.

Bluetooth

Bluetooth is used only to connect to your own OUI-Spy board and receive detections from it. The app does not connect to or control any other device.

Location

If you grant location access, your approximate position is used only on your device, to pin detections on the map and to fill in the location column when you choose to export a log. Your location is never sent to us or anyone else.

Maps

When you open the map, the map images are loaded from a map provider (OpenStreetMap on Android, Apple Maps on iOS). Like any web request, that provider can see your device's IP address and which area of the map you are viewing. No detections, identifiers, or other app data are ever attached to those requests.

Data you export

You can export your detection log as a CSV file. That file is created on your device and shared only through your phone's standard share sheet, to wherever you choose to send it. The app does not upload it anywhere.

Third parties

The app has no advertising, no analytics, and no tracking. The only outside service it uses is the map provider that supplies map tiles, described above. It talks to no other server.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue at github.com/soyboi1312/all-cameras-are-beacons.
